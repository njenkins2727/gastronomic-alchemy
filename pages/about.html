About me

Hello, good to meet you!

I'm Nathan and this is my recipe blog

Welcome to Gastronomic Alchemy! I'm thrilled to embark on this culinary journey with you. This blog is born out of a profound love for all things food and cooking—a passion that I've carried with me for as long as I can remember.

goal here is simple: I want to share the joy of cooking and eating delicious meals with all of you. Beyond just providing recipes, I aim to create a vibrant, welcoming community where everyone, regardless of their culinary expertise, can feel confident and inspired in the kitchen.

My favourite meals are most asain cuisne such as curries, noodle and rice dishes. However coconut truly does have a special place in my palette. Some of the best food experiences I've had were in Japan. A small michilin star ramen resturant had blown all expectations out of the water. Serinkans shown in the show Ugly delicious was also the best pizza ive had, so simple yet effective. What I enjoy other than cooking and eating? Right now would be bouldering, coding, beatbox and reading manga!
